Discussions to have before starting

How does a mirror work?

Make a student stand in front of the mirror and talk about what the basic properties of a mirror are through small experiments. Make them walk away from the mirror. What do they see? (The iamge is as far as the object is from the mirror) Wave at the mirror. Which hand is waving (Lateral inversion)
